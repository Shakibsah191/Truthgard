TruthGuard only processes the article text or URL you submit for analysis. We do not collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers unless you create an account. Account data (name, email, hashed password) is stored securely and never shared.

How Your Content Is Used

Submitted text and URLs are sent to our backend and processed through the Groq AI API for analysis. We do not store your submitted content after the analysis response is returned. Your content is never used to train AI models or shared with advertisers.

Third-Party Services

We use the Groq AI API to power analysis features. Content you submit is subject to Groq's data handling policies during processing. We do not sell or rent your data to any third party under any circumstances.
